Our Work

Egna Le Egna Center

The center conducts many HIV/AIDS prevention efforts with multiple partners, including home-based care to patients; care, treatment and education for orphans and vulnerable children; and education and discussion with sex workers.

Computer Lab

The Egna Le Egna computer lab is a multi-functional space, facilitating peer-learning and computer skills training, multi-media information center, and a vehicle for empowering entrepreneurial skills and building confidence in youth.

Library

The Egna Le Egna library is a resource for youth seeking the use of textbooks relevant to their schoolwork, encouraging reading and appreciation of the humanities, and providing a safe space for youth learning.

Physical Activities

As well as indoor and outdoor games, the Egna Le Egna center provides a space for youth to develop their physical fitness capabilies and self-confidence through sports, martial arts, the use of the fitness center equipment.

Music

Musical equipment and the music space at the Egna Le Egna center is used regularly to build music skills among the youth, but also to provide a creative and positive outlet for their after-school activities.

Environment

The connections between health, community, and environment are strong ones - through protecting and supporting a healthy environment, Egna Le Egna also builds community.


Outdoor Stage

The stage at Egna Le Egna is frequently used to showcase youth talents, from plays and theatrical events, to musical performances and talent shows. These provide opportunities for youth to build skills and confidence.

Educational Efforts

In addition to supporting youth development and empowerment, Egna Le Egna conducts many trainings each year around HIV/AIDS prevention, nutrition, capicity building, and adolescents social and reproductive health.
