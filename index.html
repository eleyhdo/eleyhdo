Empowering Youth

The Egna Le Egna Youth Health and Development Organization (ELEYHDO) is working to create a healthy and productive youth endowed with self-confidence and entrepreneurship in Ethiopia. We accomplish this mission through education, STI prevention, a vibrant youth center with many activities, nutrition and health efforts, and strong community partnerships.

About ELEYHDO

Adolescent Health

Egna Le Egna works actively in the area of adolescent reproductive health, HIV/AIDS prevention, care and support, family planning, economic empowerment of unemployed youth and nutrition promotion. ELEYHDO works in partnership with community leaders dedicated to breaking the silence and stigma surrounding HIV/AIDS and taking action to stop its spread through an integrated, community-based approach.
